* What is Virtual Labs?
   [[http://www.vlabs.ac.in][Virtual Labs]] is an initiative of Ministry of Human Resources, India.
   Virtual Labs is intended to augment the learning of engineering subjects
   through performing experiments.  The experiments are designed either as
   simulations or as remote triggered.  A remote triggered lab allows a user to
   connect to real equipment using a web browser.  There are currently around
   150 and labs and 1500 experiments at various stages of development and
   deployment.

* Development

   Virtual Labs Development is an effort involving

   1. development of platform and services that support deployment of labs

      and

   2. development of virtual labs
